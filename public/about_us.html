    How do I join a tournament?
    Super easy! Just create an account, find a tournament you like, and click the "Join" button to get started.

    Play your best, climb the leaderboard, and finish the tournament in a top spot to win real cash or other rewards.

    Rewards are sent directly to your linked bank account after you win. Easy, fast, and secure!
